World Religions Research

Name of the Religion Confucianism
# of followers in the world 6.1 million
% of world population 0.09%
Countries/regions where it is found China,east asia
Rituals and Sacred Objects Having peace and harmony in the world
Divinity (God or Gods, higher power) No god in confucianism but a force called tao
Scriptures (sacred texts) The four books of confucianism and the 5 classics
Salvation (deliverance from suffering/ sin/ consequences) If the mandate of heaven was broken the society will not have harmony and nature disasters will occur.
Good and Evil (ethics, virtues, how to cultivate good, how to resist evil and avoid suffering) To follow the mandate of heaven, and treat others with respect to having harmony in society.
Additional Facts Confucianism was created by an ancient philosopher named Confucius.
